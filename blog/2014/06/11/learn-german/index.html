Learn German

Jun 11th, 2014

The Alphabet

A to Z, plus the following:

  • Ä ä
  • Ö ö
  • Ü ü
  • ß

SCH

  • Fisch: fish

EI

Pronounced [ai]

  • Eis: ice
  • Reis: rice

IE

Pronounced [i:]

  • die: the
  • Diesel: diesel
  • sieben: seven

AU

  • Haus: house
  • Maus: mouse
  • laut: loud
  • aus: out

W

Pronounced [v]

  • Wolf: wolf
  • Winter: winter
  • Wagen: car, wagon

V

Pronounced [f]

  • Volk: people
  • Volkswagen [ˈfɔlksˌvaːɡən]

S

in front of a vowel

Pronounced [z]

  • sieben: seven
  • Sonntag: sunday
  • Sommer: summer
  • sauer: sour

ST, SP at beginning of a word

Pronounced as scht, schp

  • Stein: stone
  • Sport: sport

in all other places

Pronounced [s]

  • das: the
  • Meister: master

Z

Pronounced [ts]

  • Zeit: time
  • Schweiz: Switzerland

CH

ACH

  • Nacht: night
  • acht: eight
  • machen: make

OCH

  • Woche: week

UCH

  • Buch: book

AUCH

  • auch: also

all other CH

  • ich: I
  • mich: me
  • Licht: light
  • Milch: milk
  • München: Munich

ß

Pronounced [s]

  • heiß: hot
  • Straße: street

-H (after vowels)

AH, EH, IH, OH, UH, ÄH, ÖH, ÜH are long vowels.

  • Bahn: railway
  • sehen: see
  • Sohn: son
  • Schuh: shoe
  • Käse: cheese
  • kühl: cool

AA, EE, OO

They are also long vowels.

  • Haar: hair
  • See: lake
  • Boot: boat

B, D, G at word end

Pronounced [p], [t], [k]

  • ob: if, whether
  • Kind: child
  • Tag: day

TSCH

  • deutsch: German

TZ

Pronounced [ts]

  • Hitze: heat
